Welcome to DevDesk Queue

DevDesk Queue is here to help Lambda students with all their coding issues and anything they might want to know.

DevDesk Queue is a quick and easy way to get that extra hand with trouble shooting or getting stuck coding.

What we do?

DevDesk Queue provides students get the help they need by interacting with staff by just creating a ticket.

Our goal is supporting and improving the experience of getting help.

DevDesk Queue allows students to submit a help desk ticket, categorize it and post it to the help channel.

How does DevDesk Queue work?

We help Lambda School Students with help tickets submitted by students for any coding isssues they are having.

DevDesk Queue allows you to keep track of your ticket as well, Our goal is to provide the fasest response for help.

Lambda students create a new help ticket with a title, description, what you've tried and a category.

We can help you with any concerns or questions you might have.

Lambda Schools golden rule "if you are stuck on a code for more then 20 mins ask for help!". Just submit a ticket and help will be on its way!
